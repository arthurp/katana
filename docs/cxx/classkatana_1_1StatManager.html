◆ PrintStats()

void katana::StatManager::PrintStats ( std::ostream &  out)
protectedvirtual

PrintStats prints statistics to a stream.

This function is called by Print and Print, in turn, is typically called during the destruction of SharedMem. All statistics are added via this class; subclasses will need to extract the data they want with ReadInt, ReadParam and ReadFP and print their own results here.
